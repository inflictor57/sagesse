About Me


I am Akingabiye Sagesse, a member of Her Dreams Count ..As a passionate Web and Graphic Designer , I specialize in creating visually stunning, user-friendly, and responsive designs. With a deep understanding of both design principles and front-end development, I work with businesses to bring their digital presence to life. I aim to deliver designs that not only capture attention but also provide seamless user experiences.



My Approach to Design I believe that design is more than just aesthetics; it's about crafting experiences. Whether it’s designing a website, a logo, or creating a brand’s visual identity, I approach each project with a focus on functionality, user experience, and visual storytelling. I use modern design tools and latest technologies like HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, and JavaScript to ensure that my designs are not only visually appealing but also technically sound. From landing pages to full-fledged eCommerce websites, I ensure that every design serves a purpose and elevates the brand’s message. My design philosophy revolves around simplicity, clarity, and innovation, ensuring your message is communicated effectively through visually appealing and highly functional designs.

As a web developer, I specialize in creating dynamic, responsive websites that deliver seamless user experiences. I work with modern technologies like HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, and React to build highly interactive web applications that meet your specific needs. With a focus on performance, I ensure your site runs smoothly across all platforms, while being mobile-responsive and user-friendly.

As a web designer, I specialize in creating visually engaging websites that are both aesthetic and user-friendly. My design process focuses on clean layouts, intuitive navigation, and a balance of modern design trends and brand identity. I prioritize responsive design, ensuring your site looks great on all devices, and optimized visuals that enhance performance and user experience.

As a graphic designer, I focus on crafting visually compelling designs that effectively communicate your brand's identity. From logo creation to marketing materials, I work to ensure each design is tailored to resonate with your audience. By combining artistic creativity with strategic design principles, I produce engaging visuals that leave a lasting impression.
